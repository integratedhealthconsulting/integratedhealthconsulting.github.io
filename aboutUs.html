Integrated Healthcare Consulting

Your partner in integrated pain care.

Integrated Healthcare Consulting is dedicated to helping institutions prepare to meet changing demands of chronic illness care, management, and patient wellness. Whether your institution is looking to improve their patient-centered approach, address inconsistencies in care, or introduce new care offerings consistent with integrated illness care, IHC is ready to help along the way. Our clients become our partners in ushering in a new era of integrated care at their clinics.

Alexis Grant is the founder of Integrated Health Consulting. She is a doctoral student in clinical health psychology with a demonstrated history of working in research and higher education. Her research interests include tech-supported health interventions, healthcare disparities, and substance use risk assessment. She is skilled in statistical analysis, research design, and writing.
