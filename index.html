Integrated Health Consulting

Your partner in integrated pain care.

Integrated Health Consulting consults for healthcare entities -- from large hospital systems to small town private practices -- on how to better serve their patients using an integrated, whole health-model of chronic pain care.

Alexis Grant, Ph.D. candidate, is the founder of this company and a third-year student in the Pain Lab and the Clinical Psychology program at IUPUI. She earned her BS in Biology and Neuroscience from Davidson College and her MA in Psychology from Montclair State University. Her research interests include psychosocial factors affecting chronic pain treatment, substance use outcomes, and racial disparities in healthcare. She is also interested in spiritual and religious competencies for mental health professionals. In her spare time, she can be found listening to podcasts, walking her dog along the Monon trail, and baking all kinds of vegan desserts.
